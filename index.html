Hello, I am Siraput! I currently live in SF Bay Area and work as a data scientist (1st hire) at Slingshot, a series A startup backed by Ribbit Capital, where I am responsible for multiple facets of data science and analytics across the company. My work centers around growth and impact - I helped grow our users 9x so far by key initiatives on growth analytics, user segmentations, ML modeling, revenue modeling and engineering platform analytics.

Previously at Robinhood, I helped develop order routing models and end-to-end analytics visibility to achieve best-in-class order execution quality. The work resulted in $200+MM benefit for customers and Robinhood revenues. I have also worked on AI-related projects in the Social Impact team at Facebook and the Machine Learning team at Schlumberger AI.

Earlier in my career, I worked with the NASA team to build a novel predictive model for real-time greenhouse gas detection and quantification using machine learning and data analytics. My publications helped inspire the field to adopt AI for methane image analysis.

The thread of my various roles is the focus on using data science to enhance and grow innovative products that have never existed before. I deeply value how my work benefits and impacts end users in real world applications. More details on my previous projects below.

  • NLP for content understanding @FACEBOOK

As a Data Science intern in the Social Impact team at Faceook, I worked on a feature in FB groups that help admins share knowledge to users in an organized way.   • MethaNet: AI algorithm for sattellite images prediction @NASA

My research derives a novel algorithm for an accurate and real-time quantification of methane gas emissions at the source level from high-resolution 2D imagery. I use Convolutional Neuron Network (CNN) with more than 100,000 synthetic images to train and apply the predictive model to NASA JPL observational data. Our model shows more than 50% improvement from previous baseline accuracy.

  • Machine Learning for tool recommendation @SCHLUMBERGER AI

I joined Machine Learning team at Schlumberger AI as an intern to work on a tool recommendation project. I use hierachical clustering to understand target users and classification models to predict which tools from among the most common 20 types to recommend to users. The model outperformed a baseline by 15% in accuracy, precision and recall.

I also participated in the company Hackathon to predict daily oil production correction factor. My model achieved more than 90% accuracy and I was the runner-up for that Hackathon.

  • COVID-19 data science challenge @CALTECH

In Spring 2020, I participated in a campus-wide data competition. My team used a wide range of alternative data (e.g. mobility, hospital beds, population density) to predict COVID-19 mortality rate in each U.S. county over a 2-week period. The main difficulty of this prediction lies in a very small training data (as COVID-19 never happened before!). Our team applied a simple statistical approach, as well as more advanced models like Neuron Network and a physics-based SEIR model. We used ensemble methods and cross-validation to minimize the loss. Our model was one of the 4 (out of 50+ teams) that consistently outperformed baseline in all three milestones during the competition.
